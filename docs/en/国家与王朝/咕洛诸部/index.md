The **Glömer Tribes** is a collective term for the numerous nomadic and fishing/hunting tribes distributed east of the Heimet Strait and north of the vast Zhao Xiasi and Anioksait forests. Despite significant differences in customs, political structures, and livelihoods due to variations in climate and resources across their territories, they share similar origin myths and linguistic systems, forming an important component of the Trusk Empire's northeastern borderlands.

## Concept and Etymology 📜

The term <ruby>Glömer<rt>Glömer</rt></ruby> originally means "by the river" in the Glömezher language.

- **Origin of the External Name**: Initially, the Truskeans used this term to refer to the earliest clan they encountered, who called themselves "Glömer." Over time, this name became fixed in the Truskean language as a general term for all related northern peoples.
- **Evolution of Self-Identity**: After centuries of exchange and integration, especially following the assimilation or disappearance of the original "Glömer" branch, other tribes gradually accepted and internalized this ethnic designation.

### Linguistic Differences
The word <ruby>Glömer<rt>Glömer</rt></ruby> contains the front vowel *ö* in its native language, which contrasts with *o*. Since Truskean lacks this sound, it is often transliterated as *Gulomer*. The exact cause of this phonetic correspondence is undetermined; academia generally believes it to be a fossilized phenomenon resulting from phonetic perception differences during the initial period of contact between the two groups.

## Geographical Environment

The territory of the Glömer people is located in the narrow corridor between the North Sea and the East Sea, with terrain sloping from high in the north to low in the south, featuring complex and diverse topography.

### Topography and Climate
- **Eastern Region (Glömer Grasslands)**:
    - **Northern Part**: Higher elevation, lush with water and grass, providing excellent pastureland.
    - **Southern Part**: Influenced by strong southward westerlies and the North Sea warm current, it is relatively warm but highly prone to flooding and storm disasters.
- **Western Region (Mistnatira Mountains)**:
    - **Northern Mountains**: Higher latitude, featuring a mixed ecological biome of snowfields, alpine coniferous forests, and tundra.
    - **Southern Mountains**: Moderated by the warm current, supporting more temperate mixed coniferous and broadleaf forests.

![trusk_eco](trusk_eco.png)

### Ecology and Wildlife
- **Vegetation**: Dominated by vast grasslands, encompassing various forms from river valley grasslands to alpine meadows. Cold-resistant tundra vegetation (mosses, lichens, dwarf shrubs) is distributed in high-altitude or high-latitude areas.
- **Animals**:
    - **Livestock**: **Cattle** and **sheep** are the foundation of food and economy; **horses** are primarily used for transportation and military activities.
    - **Wildlife**: **Wolf packs** are the main terrestrial predators; **eagles** occupy the sky's ecological niche; **foxes** and **wild dogs** are widely distributed across various habitats.

## Historical Development

### Origin Myth
The early history of the <ruby>Glömer<rt>Glömer</rt></ruby> people is unverifiable due to a lack of written records characteristic of nomadic societies. According to [Glömer Legends](咕洛传说.md), the Glömer people were created from the flesh and blood of the goddess **Lamruo** and survived the cataclysm under the protection of the sacred mountain **Karoda**.

### Establishment of the <ruby>Moswarike<rt>Moswarike</rt></ruby> Dynasty
The first "unified" period of the Glömer people is known as the **Moswarike Dynasty** (meaning Horse Clan Dynasty), founded by the legendary leader <ruby>Guuchobagu<rt>Guuchobagu</rt></ruby>.

- **Rise**: <ruby>Guuchobagu<rt>Guuchobagu</rt></ruby> was originally a survivor who had been defeated in steppe conflicts and enslaved. He utilized religious superstition and personal spiritual power to unite the lower-class slaves of various tribes during a harsh winter.
- **Seizure of Power**: At a major tribal gathering, he launched a coup in the "name of Latroda," purging the drunken elder class. By promising wealth (cattle and sheep) and emancipation for slaves, he successfully integrated the military and major tribes.
- **Rule**: He proclaimed himself the <b><ruby>Sajepyomak<rt>Sajepyomak</rt></ruby></b> (Great Elder of the World). Although this unification was largely nominal, with actual power still constrained by the Great Elder Council and local clans, he successfully awakened the national consciousness of the Glömer people.

### Succession Crisis and Southern Flight
Guuchobagu died without an heir, and his brother <ruby>Onguchotro<rt>Onguchotro</rt></ruby> succeeded him. After Onguchotro's death, the Great Elder Council refused to recognize the succession rights of his son <ruby>Onguchotronor<rt>Onguchotronor</rt></ruby>, instead installing a collateral line. Onguchotronor's resistance failed, forcing him to flee south to Zhao Xiasi.

### Dynasty Restoration and Reforms
During his exile, Onguchotronor, with the help of loyal border tribes and advanced equipment obtained from the Truskeans, launched a counteroffensive, defeated the Great Elder Council, and established the sacred rule of the Moswarike family.

**Major Reform Measures**:
1.  **Political Centralization**: Drawing on Trusk institutions, he weakened the actual power of the Great Elder Council, downgrading it to an advisory body.
2.  **Magical Revival**: He established the <b><ruby>Johuzay<rt>Johuzay</rt></ruby></b> (Priest) system. Taking advantage of the favorable timing when the "snapshot" principle was still distant, he established a mature "Spirit Language" magic system.
3.  **Cultural Surpassing**: As the Truskeans were embroiled in wars with the Laprio people during the same period, leading to the destruction of their典籍, the Glömer people's mastery of the Spirit Language一度 surpassed that of the south, laying the foundation for the later establishment of the Glömer Dynasty in the western territories of the Trusk Empire.

## Political and Social System ⚖️

### Basic Structure
Due to their nomadic nature, the political units of the Glömer people were primarily based on geographical distribution and blood relations, resulting in a relatively loose structure.

- **<b><ruby>Clan<rt>Krota</rt></ruby></b>**: The basic political unit (e.g., mountain clan, river clan).
- **<b><ruby>Family<rt>Shnomca</rt></ruby></b>**: The basic social cell, practicing patriarchy, with family members collectively owning livestock and tents.
- **<b><ruby>Elder Council<rt>Pyomakzherchi</rt></ruby></b>**: The decision-making body of each clan. A higher-level <b><ruby>Great Elder Council<rt>Lapyomokzhorchi</rt></ruby></b> existed among the clans.

### Evolution of Titles
- **<b><ruby>Elder<rt>Pyomak</rt></ruby></b>**: Originally referred to clan leaders, later gradually evolved into a term for nobility.
- **<b><ruby>Sajepyomak<rt>Sajepyomak</rt></ruby></b>** (World Elder): The title for the supreme ruler of the Glömer people. After incorporation into the Trusk Empire, it was used to refer to the Empire's Emperor.
- **<b><ruby>Jupyomak<rt>Jupyomak</rt></ruby></b>** (Sun Elder): The专用称呼 for the Glömer King during the imperial period.

### Religion and Spirit Speakers
The Glömer people did not develop a严密 religious organization; their belief system was primarily maintained by <b><ruby>Spirit Speakers<rt>Johuzay</rt></ruby></b>.
- **Function**: Those proficient in the <b><ruby>Spirit Language<rt>Johuz</rt></ruby></b> were called Spirit Speakers, possessing abilities in prophecy, healing, and communicating with spirits.
- **Inheritance**: They formed a loose noble class, with knowledge passed down strictly orally,严禁书面记录.

## Economy and Culture

### Economic Model
- **Animal Husbandry**: Centered on herding yaks, goats, and other livestock, producing fur, tent materials, clothing, and staple food.
- **Agriculture**: Limited cultivated land was developed near rivers, primarily growing cold-resistant cereal crops as a food supplement.

### Cultural Characteristics
- **Beliefs**: A融合 of deep ancestor worship and primitive religious beliefs.
- **Language**: Historically, there was no unified standard official language, mainly divided into coastal and inland dialects. The documented **Glömezher language** (see the [[docs/zh/语言/图斯克/咕洛语|Glömezher]] entry) is primarily based on the southern coastal dialect.
