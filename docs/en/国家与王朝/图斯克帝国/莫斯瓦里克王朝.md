The <b><ruby>Moswarike<rt>Moswarike</rt></ruby></b> Dynasty, also referred to in historiography as the **Glömezher Dynasty** or the **Horse Clan Dynasty**, was a unified empire in Truskean history established by the northern nomadic [Glömezher Tribes](docs/zh/国家与王朝/咕洛诸部/index.md).

The dynasty's founder was the tenth Great Chieftain of the Glömezher people, [Tuluhaihe](图录亥刻.md). The establishment of the Moswarike Dynasty marked the first time the northern nomadic civilization comprehensively conquered and effectively ruled the southern agrarian and maritime civilizations. This period was characterized by its powerful "Spirit Tongue" magic legions, unique slave-guard system, and a harsh intelligence network for governance. Although its administrative methods were relatively crude, objectively, the Moswarike Dynasty greatly promoted the bloodline integration and cultural amalgamation of the northern and southern peoples of the Trusk Empire.

## 📜 Historical Development

### Rise and Unification
The dynasty began with the unification movement led by [Tuluhaihe](图录亥刻.md). As an outstanding leader in Glömezher history, he ended the long-standing tribal conflicts on the northern steppes, unified the various tribes, and was elected as the tenth Great Chieftain (Sajepiomak). In the early years of his rule, Tuluhaihe adopted an extremely pragmatic and low-profile diplomatic strategy, ostensibly paying tribute and pledging allegiance to the then southern hegemon to lull his opponents, while secretly strengthening his military and conducting profound reforms to the traditional nomadic military system.

### Southern Conquest

When the time was ripe, Tuluhaihe led the reformed Glömezher army south. In the decisive battle against the southern [Basib Dynasty](../../../zh/国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/巴西卜王朝.md), the Glömezher forces demonstrated overwhelming military superiority. In addition to traditional elite cavalry, Tuluhaihe deployed "Spirit Tongue" (mage) units on a large scale. Taking advantage of the period when the world's planar "snapshot" was far from the origin point and environmental magical tides were high, the storms and lightning summoned by the Glömezher successfully shattered the southern allied forces' defensive lines.

Subsequently, the Moswarike army captured the strategic city of [Hasit](../../地理/图斯克/黑蔑特/哈希特城.md), entered the [Heimet region](../../地理/图斯克/黑蔑特/index.md), and continued pushing south, ultimately conquering vast territories including the [Trusk Basin](../../地理/图斯克/index.md), establishing a vast empire.

### Decline and End
The later period of Moswarike Dynasty rule faced an irreversible dual crisis:

*   **"Magic" Decline**: As the world's plane shifted (snapshot regression), the ambient spiritual tides began to recede. The effectiveness of the Spirit Tongue techniques, upon which the dynasty relied for its military deterrence, significantly weakened, leading to a decline in its absolute coercive power over various regions.
*   **Internal Corruption and Division**: The Glömezher nobles who migrated south were rapidly corrupted by the influence of the local lifestyle, while simultaneously developing serious political rifts with the conservative factions remaining in the north.

Ultimately, amidst uprisings across the land and the resurgence of old noble forces, this dynasty built on military might and magic came to an end.

## ⚖️ Political and Military Systems

### Hybrid Administrative System
Tuluhaihe implemented a reformed feudal system. He granted conquered lands to meritorious generals and compliant southern nobles in exchange for a foundation of rule; however, in the empire's core regions, the dynasty maintained a highly centralized and oppressive control.

### Slave-Soldier System
To weaken and counter the power of traditional clan elders, Tuluhaihe abolished the monopoly of tribal militias. He established a unique slave-soldier system, vigorously guaranteeing the basic rights of slaves and selecting the strongest from among them to form a direct standing army. This slave legion operated independently of the elder council system, swore allegiance solely to the emperor, possessed high combat effectiveness and discipline, and served as the cornerstone of the dynasty.

### Secret Police Rule: Molofa
The dynasty established a direct intelligence and surveillance agency named "Molofa" (meaning "arrow"). This organization reported directly to the emperor and was responsible for monitoring the vast bureaucracy and local lords. Through its pervasive spy network, the central government strengthened its control over the extensive territories, maintaining a rule based on a balance of terror.

## 🏺 Society and Culture

*   **Extensive Governance**: As a conquering regime, the Moswarike Dynasty's administration was relatively extensive. Many of the South's originally sophisticated agricultural systems and commercial networks were damaged in the initial conquest period, only gradually recovering their vitality by the mid-to-late dynasty.
*   **Great Ethnic Fusion**: To consolidate its rule, the dynasty encouraged and even enforced intermarriage between the Glömezher and Truskean peoples. This policy led to a significant influx of Glömezher vocabulary into the Truskean language, and the lifestyles of the North and South underwent irreversible and deep integration during this period.
*   **Golden Age of Spirit Tongue Technology**: Benefiting from the high magical tides and imperial patronage, this period was the peak of Spirit Tongue research. The imperial court funded numerous research projects on magic, astrology, and herbalism, and many classic works on the Spirit Tongue were compiled during this time.
