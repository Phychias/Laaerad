# <ruby>Jaudzya<rt>jaudziya</rt></ruby> Dynasty

## Jaudzya Dynasty

The Jaudzya Dynasty was established after the long-dormant Jaudzya army captured Heimet during the late period of the [Moswarike Dynasty](莫斯瓦里克王朝.md), a time marked by the decline of "magic" and chaos within the imperial court. The first monarch, Tika I, attempted to promote centralizing reforms. However, his son, Yechika, emerged victorious in the power struggle and dismantled his father's vision through enfeoffment, leading to the collapse of the reforms. After the death of Jaudzya Yechika, the state fragmented, and Trusk once again descended into civil war.
