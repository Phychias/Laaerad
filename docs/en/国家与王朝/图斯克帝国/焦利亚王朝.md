The Joria Dynasty was a significant period in the history of the Tusk Empire. It was established after the Joria army, which had been dormant for many years, captured Heimiette during the late [Mosvarik Dynasty](莫斯瓦里克王朝.md), a time marked by the decline of "magic" and chaos in the imperial court.

## History 📜

The first monarch, **Tika I**, attempted to promote centralization reforms, but his son **Yechika** emerged victorious in the subsequent power struggle. After ascending to power, Yechika undermined his father's centralization vision through a policy of enfeoffment, ultimately leading to the failure of the reforms. After the death of **Joria Yechika**, the dynasty lost its cohesion, the state rapidly fragmented, and the Tusk Empire once again descended into prolonged civil war.
