The **Tyesuku Dynasty** was a highly influential ruling period in the history of the Tusk region. Among them, the Tyesuku Second Dynasty was particularly crucial. Through a series of top-down, in-depth reforms, this period successfully laid the foundation for the empire's centralized rule, profoundly influencing the political landscape of later generations.

## ⚔️ Tyesuku Second Dynasty

The Tyesuku Second Dynasty was established by the leader of the [Tyesuku](docs/zh/人物/铁苏库/index.md) family, [Dezhuohei III](德卓黑三世.md), leading an uprising. After successfully capturing the imperial capital, the new regime did not follow the old system but instead carried out a comprehensive and profound restructuring of the empire's administrative, military, and economic systems.

### Administrative System

Regarding the inheritance of legitimacy, the dynasty clearly established the **primogeniture system** and strictly stipulated that if a lord died without an heir, their fief must be reclaimed by the central government, thereby weakening local separatist forces. In terms of the specific administrative structure, the dynasty implemented differentiated management based on geopolitics:

- **Heimet Principality**: Located in the core area surrounding the capital [Heimet](../../地理/图斯克/黑蔑特/index.md). This region was governed by the emperor himself or a trusted imperial prince serving as the **<b>Prince of Heimet</b>**, implementing highly unified direct administrative management.
- **Tyesuku Kingdom**: As the old territory of the [Tyesuku](docs/zh/人物/铁苏库/index.md) family, it was governed by a member of the imperial collateral line serving as the [King of Tyesuku](docs/zh/人物/铁苏库/index.md), retaining the tradition of the enfeoffment system.
- **Laprio Region**: Continued to be governed by the local [King of Laprio](../../地理/拉普里奥/index.md), maintaining relative independence.
- **Southern Colonies**: A new **Annan Administrative Office** was established for specialized management, reflecting the administrative will for southern expansion.
- **Guluo Tribes**: Led by the King of Guluo, each clan was allowed to retain its original tribal customs or early feudal systems.

### Military System

To consolidate its rule, the dynasty adopted a diversified military organizational structure to meet the defense needs of different regions:

- **Capital Region**: Implemented a strict system of separation of military powers to prevent any single general from amassing excessive military strength.
- **[Guluo Tribes](docs/zh/国家与王朝/咕洛诸部/index.md)**: Retained their traditional tribal military system.
- **Various Kingdoms**: Generally established permanent garrisons. Among them, the [Heimet region](../../地理/图斯克/黑蔑特/index.md) was completely dominated by central permanent troops, while other regions still relied mainly on feudal conscripts.

### Economic System

Given that prolonged turmoil had caused the collapse of the original unified monetary system in Tusk society, the dynasty, assessing the situation, implemented differentiated economic policies tailored to local conditions:

- **Heimet Region**: The imperial court restored the right to mint coins, implemented a **unified monetary tax system**, and began levying taxes on commercial transactions, rebuilding financial order.
- **[Guluo Tribes](docs/zh/国家与王朝/咕洛诸部/index.md)**: Limited by their level of economic development, they still paid taxes in the form of **barter exchange**.
- **Other Regions**: Due to the monetary system still awaiting restoration, the policy of levying **taxes in kind** was primarily implemented.

### Historical Significance

The reign of the Tyesuku Second Dynasty was of landmark significance. Although significant differences in government forms, customs, and cultural backgrounds still existed among the various parts of the [Tusk region](../../地理/图斯克/index.md), this marked the first time the region was systematically integrated into a unified, centralized government framework. This series of reforms not only ended the prolonged chaos but also marked the formal establishment of the **<b>"true empire"</b>** on the land of Tusk.
