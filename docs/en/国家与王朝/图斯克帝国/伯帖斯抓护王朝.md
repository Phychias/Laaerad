## The Portesdrahu Dynasty

The Portesdrahu Dynasty was a dynasty of the Trusk Empire. It ended the Third Dynasty of Heimet through an assassination, ushering in an era where Glömezher nobility ruled the empire. This dynasty greatly promoted ethnic integration within the Trusk Empire.

## References

Portesdrahu is a concept in the world of Laaerad. It can refer to a family surname within the Trusk Empire, or to a dynasty of the Trusk Empire.

### Etymology

Portesdrahu is a word that exists in both Glömezher and Truskean, originating from Proto-Northeastern Laaerad. *Portes* in Northeastern Laaerad likely means "foresight" or "precognition," while "drahu" is a Glömezher suffix used to derive occupational nouns that take an object. In summary, the meaning of Portesdrahu approximates "prophet" or "seer."
