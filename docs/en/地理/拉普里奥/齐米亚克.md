<b><ruby>Cimiyak<rt>Cimiyak</rt></ruby></b> is a region located in the southwestern frontier of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md). This area is administratively and geopolitically part of the <b><ruby>Trusk<rt>Trusk</rt></ruby></b> Empire. Its borderlands are adjacent to a vast, seemingly endless great desert, forming a significant geographical barrier in the southwestern direction of the empire.

## 🏜️ Geographical Environment

As a frontier territory of the Trusk Empire, Cimiyak's geographical features are primarily characterized by the convergence of borderlands and desert:

- **Frontier Location**: Situated at the southwestern edge of the known map, far from the central heartland.
- **Desert Border**: The region directly borders a vast desert, and its natural environment is significantly influenced by the desert climate.
