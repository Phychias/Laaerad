This document aims to systematically outline the geographic framework of the world of Laaerad 🗺️. It primarily covers the hierarchical division of geographic concepts, the distribution of core regions, and a detailed list of major cities, passes, towns, villages, and ports.

## Trusk Region Geographic Common Name System

This section lists the common geographic administrative hierarchy names used in the Trusk region. These terms form the foundational suffixes for local place names, aiding in understanding the etymology and scale of toponyms.

- <b><ruby>天下<rt>Tamokuirad</rt></ruby></b>: The highest-level geographic concept, referring to the entire world.
    - <b><ruby>王国<rt>Kuirad</rt></ruby></b>: The primary political entity unit.
        - <b><ruby>地区<rt>Layasli</rt></ruby></b>: A broad geographic subdivision under a kingdom.
            - <b><ruby>城<rt>Wies</rt></ruby></b>: A large population center and administrative hub.
                - <b><ruby>镇<rt>Adeis</rt></ruby></b>: A medium or small settlement, often with a specific function (e.g., military or trade).
                - <b><ruby>殿<rt>Dungoi</rt></ruby></b>: A sacred site or place of worship with religious significance.
                - <b><ruby>堡<rt>Gordum</rt></ruby></b>: A military fortress with defensive functions.
                - <b><ruby>港<rt>Abaj</rt></ruby></b>: A hub for waterway transportation.
                    - <b><ruby>道<rt>Lous</rt></ruby></b>: A major land transportation route.
                    - <b><ruby>航道<rt>Valos</rt></ruby></b>: A major waterway shipping route.
