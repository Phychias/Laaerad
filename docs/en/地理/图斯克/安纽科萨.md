**Anioksait** is a vast tropical rainforest region located on the eastern continent of [Laaerad](../拉埃拉德.md) 🌿. Serving as the natural eastern border of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md), this jungle is renowned for its impassable geography, marking the natural terminus of the empire's territorial expansion.

## Geographic Features

Anioksait is situated on the eastern fringe of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md) and possesses a typical tropical rainforest ecosystem. The region experiences a hot and humid climate with extremely high and dense vegetation coverage. This intricate natural environment forms a formidable physical barrier, making conventional travel and traversal exceptionally difficult, thereby constituting an insurmountable obstacle on the empire's eastern frontier.

## Historical Significance

Historically, the Anioksait jungle defined the eastern limit of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md)'s expansion.

During the reign of the [Heimet Dynasty](黑蔑特王朝.md), despite the empire's proactive expansionist policies and continuous eastward push, its advance ultimately halted at the edge of Anioksait's forest sea. As large armies could not effectively penetrate this primeval rainforest, the empire's territorial expansion was forced to cease here. This geographical constraint objectively shaped the empire's territorial form and indirectly influenced the dynasty's subsequent strategic planning and developmental direction.
