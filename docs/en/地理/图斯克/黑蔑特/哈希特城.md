# Hasit City

**Hasit City** is the political, economic, and cultural center of the Hasit region. The city is currently located on the south side of the Serk Mountains, adjacent to Heimet Bay, serving as a crucial hub within the Empire's territory.

## Historical Development

The city was originally established along the banks of the old Pernan River. After being destroyed by severe flooding, the original site was abandoned, and the city was subsequently relocated and rebuilt on the more stable southern foothills of the Serk Mountains.

## The Matasioksamen Reform

During the **Matasioksamen Reform**, authorities constructed the [Matasioksamen Avenue](马塔肖萨门大道.md) connecting Hasit City with the [Tusk Basin](../index.md). This project greatly facilitated the transportation of goods and the flow of information, further solidifying Heimet's position as a core region of the Empire.
