# Moswarike Clan

The <b><ruby>Moswarike<rt>Moswarike</rt></ruby></b> Clan is a political entity of milestone significance in the historical progression of the Guluo tribes. As the core force that ended the era of tribal division and established a unified regime, this clan laid the foundation for the later <b><ruby>Moswarike<rt>Moswarik </rt></ruby></ruby></b> Dynasty 🏰. Historically, through political integration and military conquest, the clan transformed the loose tribal confederation into a centralized dynastic system.

## Key Figures

- [Turhaike](图录亥刻.md): Revered as the tenth-generation Great Chieftain of the Guluo tribes. With exceptional leadership, he achieved the formal unification of the entire Guluo territory and officially inaugurated the era of Moswarike Dynasty rule, serving as the key founder of the clan's prosperity.
