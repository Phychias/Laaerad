# Tuluhaike

**Tuluhaike** was the tenth great chieftain of the [[Moswarike Dynasty]]. He consolidated and unified the [[docs/zh/国家与王朝/咕洛诸部/index|Guluo Tribes]], establishing a powerful and united army. He implemented a feudal system, safeguarded the rights of slaves and established a slave-soldier system, while secretly pledging allegiance to the Heimet central government to lull them into complacency. He established the intelligence agency "Molofa Organization" and an army independent of the Council of Elders to strengthen his rule. In the war against Basib, he achieved victory with the help of Spirit Speakers, entered Heimet, and conquered most of the regions south of the Truskean Basin. His name "Tuluhaike" means "combative" in Truskean, a nickname he earned for his fierce fighting on the battlefield.
