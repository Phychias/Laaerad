<b><ruby>图录亥刻<rt>Turhaike</rt></ruby></b> was the tenth Great Chieftain of the [Moswarike Clan](index.md), renowned for his exceptional political acumen and military integration capabilities. As an extremely ambitious ruler, he successfully achieved the formal unification of the perpetually warring [Guluo tribes](../../国家与王朝/咕洛诸部/index.md) and established a highly centralized and formidable military system. During his reign, he consolidated royal authority by implementing a feudal system and a slave soldier system, expanding his sphere of influence into <b><ruby>黑蔑特<rt>Heimet</rt></ruby></b> and the southern part of the Truskean Basin.

## 📜 Origin of the Name

"Turhaike" is not his original name but an honorific title derived from the Truskean language, meaning <b>"combative"</b>. This title originated from his fierce and tireless fighting style on the battlefield in his early years, gradually becoming his most representative appellation.

## Political Governance and Reforms

Turhaike's ruling style combined pragmatism and cunning. He implemented a series of reform measures in domestic and foreign affairs aimed at strengthening centralization.

### Restructuring of Power
To break free from the constraints of traditional tribal politics, Turhaike took the following measures:

- **Establishment of an Independent Army**: He created a standing armed force directly loyal to the Great Chieftain himself, independent of the traditional council of elders, thereby securing the stability of his rule on a physical level.
- **Construction of an Intelligence Network**: He founded the secret intelligence organization named <b><ruby>末洛法<rt>Molofa</rt></ruby></b>. This agency permeated all levels of the tribes, responsible for monitoring dissidents and gathering intelligence, greatly enhancing control over the territory.

### Social System Reforms
- **Implementation of a Feudal System**: By redefining spheres of influence and redistributing interests, he integrated the leaders of various tribes into a unified management system.
- **Slave Soldier System**: He unprecedentedly implemented policies protecting the rights of slaves and, based on this, established the <b>slave soldier system</b>. This not only expanded the military manpower but also cultivated an elite force fiercely loyal to the monarch due to gratitude and dependency.

### Diplomatic Strategy
Facing the powerful [Heimet](../../地理/图斯克/黑蔑特/index.md), Turhaike adopted a strategy of "biding one's time and concealing one's strength." He ostensibly pledged allegiance in secret, paying tribute on time, thereby lulling the enemy and reducing their vigilance towards the rise of the Guluo tribes. This bought him precious time and space for his own military expansion.

## ⚔️ Military Expansion

Turhaike's military career was marked by numerous victories. His expansionist wars fundamentally altered the surrounding geopolitical landscape.

- **Unification of the Guluo Tribes**: He ended the prolonged inter-tribal warfare, achieving the formal political and military unification of the Guluo tribes.
- **Conquest of Basib**: In the decisive battle against his formidable rival <b><ruby>巴西卜<rt>Basib</rt></ruby></b>, Turhaike cleverly utilized the supernatural power of the Spirit Tongue to secure a decisive victory.
- **Taking Control of Heimet**: When the time was ripe, he led his forces north to take control of Heimet and successfully conquered the vast territories south of the Truskean Basin, establishing the hegemonic status of the Moswarike Dynasty.
