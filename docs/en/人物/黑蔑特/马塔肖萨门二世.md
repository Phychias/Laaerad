# <ruby>Matasioksaamen<rt>matasioksaamen</rt></ruby> II

Son of [[Matasioksaamen I]], the first monarch of the Heimet First Dynasty. During his reign, he continued his father's policies, developed the empire's economy, and further consolidated the political system of the Tusk core region and the ruling legitimacy of the Heimet family. He is a rather important monarch in Tusk history.

## Biography

Year of birth unknown.

In 883 BC, he assassinated the sole male heir of the Quileo family, then seized power as the son-in-law of the Quileo family. By winter, he usurped the throne and ascended.

During his reign, he continued most of his father's policies, further advancing the reform of the Tusk state's administrative system and implementing a bureaucratic system locally. He constructed the Port of Borlasom in the gulf between Turha and Heimet.

Died in 870 BC, cause of death **unknown**.
