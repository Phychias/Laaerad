<ruby><b>Porlaspon</b><rt>Porlaspon</rt></ruby> is a legendary military commander and conqueror in Tusk's history. As the illegitimate son of Matasioksamen III, he grew up in the shadows and discrimination of the court, which shaped his cold and resilient character and drove him to win recognition and power through illustrious military achievements.

## Biography

After growing weary of the internal power struggles at the Hasit court, Porlaspon turned his gaze to the uncivilized lands to the south. He personally led his army to force a crossing of the Krusofa River three times, ultimately completely defeating the alliance of indigenous tribes entrenched in the region, becoming the Conqueror of the South.

To consolidate his gains, Porlaspon established the strategically important military town of **Tupelo** (meaning "Victory") on the middle reaches of the Krusofa River. Unlike the commercial center of Hasit, Tupelo was built entirely according to military defensive principles, with its street layout focused on the needs of urban warfare. It gradually developed into the core base for Porlaspon's rule and further expeditions.

Furthermore, Porlaspon expanded Tusk's sphere of influence on the eastern bank of Novaya, establishing stable colonial settlements and military outposts. His most significant achievement was the conquest of the Rui Lian territory, where rich gold deposits were discovered. The continuous flow of gold back to Hasit greatly consolidated his political position and temporarily quelled disputes at court regarding his background.
