# The Heimet Family

**The Heimet Family** is a powerful noble family that has repeatedly appeared throughout the history of [Tusk](../../../en/概念/图斯克.md). Its rise is closely linked to the prosperity of commerce and trade. During the late period of the Quileo Dynasty, the city of Hashet became a significant commercial center due to its advantageous port conditions, which allowed the Heimet family to amass substantial economic power.
