 If the Heimet family is the skeleton of the Trusk Empire, then House Visyufon is the flesh and nerves attached to it. They do not seek to wield the scepter on the stage, but are passionate about deciding who holds it behind the scenes. Throughout many dynasties in Trusk history, their influence has extended everywhere in the shadow of the imperial throne.
