# Oleman

!!! wiki "Character Profile"
    
    |   |   |
    |---|---|
    | Full Name | Visyufon Oleman |
    | **Title** | Chief Senator of the Senate, Leader of the Republican Faction |
    | **Active Era** | Republican Period (approx. 660 - 590 BCE) |
    | **Key Deeds** | Maintaining the "Rule of the Seven Families," striking a restoration deal with Zhuoxi |
    | **Political Stance** | Extreme pragmatist, defender of aristocratic privileges |

> "Without an emperor, Tusk still functions; but without the nobility, Tusk would descend into barbarism."

Oleman is the most elegant politician in the family's history. During the "Rule of the Seven Families" era following the collapse of imperial power, as the leader of the most powerful family, he had the opportunity to proclaim himself emperor. However, he chose to maintain the balance of the Senate, securing tangible benefits by controlling taxation and land.

Faced with the rise of the political genius Zhuoxi, Oleman keenly realized that the republican system could no longer cope with external threats. He demonstrated remarkable political flexibility, choosing to betray his republican allies and reaching a secret pact with Zhuoxi: the Visyufon family would support the restoration of the monarchy on the condition that the new regime must codify a series of aristocratic privileges into law. This deal allowed the family to emerge unscathed, even more prosperous, from the dynastic transition.
