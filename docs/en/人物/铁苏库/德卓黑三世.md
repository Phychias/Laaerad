**Dezhorhei III was a monarch of epoch-making significance in the history of the Trusk Empire** and the founder of the **Second Tyesuku Dynasty**. He not only ended the empire's prolonged chaos through armed uprising and established a centralized political structure but is also regarded as a key promoter of the empire's cultural revival due to his staunch support for the excavation of ancient ruins and linguistic research during his reign.

## 👑 Political and Military Achievements

As the leader of the <b><ruby>Tyesuku<rt>Tesuku</rt></ruby></b> family, Dezhorhei III led an uprising that captured the old imperial capital and established the Second Tyesuku Dynasty. His rule marked the first time the Trusk region was integrated into a government framework with genuine centralization.

- **Establishment of Centralized Legal Authority**: Implemented a strict **primogeniture inheritance system** and decreed that fiefdoms must revert to the central authority upon the extinction of a lord's lineage, effectively weakening local separatist forces.
- **Differentiated Administrative Management**:
    - Established the **Heimet Principality** around the capital, directly governed by the imperial family;
    - In frontier regions such as **Laprio** and the **Glömezher tribes**, relatively independent kingship or tribal systems were retained, demonstrating flexible geopolitical wisdom.
- **Military Restructuring**: Implemented a separation of military powers within the capital region to prevent the concentration of military authority, while establishing a central defense system led by a permanent standing army, changing the old system that relied solely on feudal levies.

## 📜 Cultural and Academic Contributions

Dezhorhei III's contributions in the cultural field are as illustrious as his political achievements. According to the *[Truskean](../../语言/图斯克/图斯克语.md)* records, inspired by the "Spirit Language," he initiated the empire's systematic research into ancient civilizations.

- **Founding of the Royal Society**: The emperor personally ordered the establishment of the **Royal Society** and set up specialized institutions to provide funding and manpower support.
- **Archaeological and Linguistic Research**: Under his impetus, the Society excavated multiple ruins, including the Glömezher stone stele of Mistnatila, the Laprio obelisk, and ancient Trusk statues. This work laid a solid foundation for deciphering **Ancient Truskean** and **Proto-Laaeradian**.
- **Impact on Governance**: The fifteen years of his reign were a golden age for imperial academic research; after his death, funding for the Society was once reduced, leading to a slowdown in the study of ancient monuments.

## 💰 Economic Reforms

Faced with economic collapse caused by war, Dezhorhei III implemented pragmatic and resolute economic policies aimed at rebuilding the empire's financial order.

- **Restoration of Coinage Rights**: The imperial court reclaimed the right to mint currency, ending the chaos in the monetary system.
- **Implementation of Tax Reform**: A unified currency tax system and commercial taxes were implemented in the core region of **Heimet**; in economically less developed areas like the **Glömezher tribes** and other regions, barter or tax-in-kind systems were temporarily maintained, reflecting a governance logic adapted to local conditions.
