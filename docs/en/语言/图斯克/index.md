**Languages of the Trusk Region** are classified in historical linguistics as the **Northeastern Laaerad Language Family**. This group of languages is distributed across the northeastern part of the Laaerad continent, covering a vast area from the [Heimet](../../地理/图斯克/黑蔑特/index.md) Strait coast to the western mountains and the Novaya Sea coast.

All languages in this family originate from the pre-Cataclysm [Proto-Laaerad](拉埃拉德原始语.md). Through prolonged geographical isolation and social differentiation, they evolved into three distinct typological features, corresponding to the three major cultural spheres of the region.

---

## 📜 Historical Evolution

The evolutionary history of the Trusk region languages typically uses the **Cataclysm** as a key dividing line, serving as a microcosm of the reconstruction process of its civilization.

### Common Origin
Before the Cataclysm, the unified [Proto-Laaerad](../拉埃拉德原始语.md) was spoken in the northeastern part of the continent. This was an ancient language with complex consonant clusters, a strict prefix chain (following the order of negation-passive-causative), and a distinction between long and short vowels.

### The Great Divergence Period
The Cataclysm led to the isolation of settlements by geographical barriers. Proto-Laaerad gradually differentiated into [[Proto-Northeastern Laaerad]], which then rapidly split into three branches:
-   **🏔️ Western Highland Branch**: Tribes in the isolated mountainous environment retained the **agglutinative** characteristics of the proto-language and, through rapid oral communication, evolved **vowel harmony**, ultimately forming [[Glömezher]].
-   **🌊 Eastern Coastal Branch**:
    -   Strait Plains: To meet the needs of military administration and efficient communication, the language underwent drastic analyticization, with complex affixes dropping off to become independent particles, forming [[Truskean]].
    -   **Novaya Sea Coast**: Influenced by maritime trade and foreign cultures, the language took a fusional path, developing unique vowel mutations and a gender system, forming [Laprio](拉普里奥语.md).

## 🗣️ Major Languages

### [[Truskean]]
!!! wiki "Truskean"
    
    | | |
    | ----------------- | -------- |
    **Typological Tendency** | Partially Agglutinative, Partially Analytic |
    **Primary Distribution** | Core regions of the Trusk Empire and various administrative centers |
    **Social Status** | Imperial official language and lingua franca |

Truskean, as the official language of the Trusk Empire, is a **highly agglutinative language** based on the Strait dialect. Its phonological structure is simple and symmetrical, with primary stress typically on the word-initial syllable. The language possesses a rigorous **noun case and definiteness system** (including five cases and a definite suffix). Verb morphology is extremely rich, using a strictly ordered chain of prefixes and suffixes to precisely control valency, voice, manner, and the categories of "tense-aspect-mood." Syntactically, Truskean exhibits typical **topic-prominent** features. While case marking allows for flexible word order, the basic structure tends towards SVO. Noun phrases strictly follow **head-initial** patterns. Additionally, the language retains a complex system of **personal pronouns and honorifics** reflecting the social hierarchy.

### [[Glömezher]]

!!! wiki "Glömezher"
    
    | | |
    | ----------------- | -------- |
    **Typological Tendency** | Agglutinative |
    **Primary Distribution** | [Mistnatila](../../地理/咕洛/米斯特那提拉.md) |
    **Social Status** | Tribal lingua franca, shamanic ritual language |

**Glömezher**, as the lingua franca of the Glömezher nomadic tribes in the northwestern Laaerad continent, profoundly reflects the cultural characteristics of this steppe people, where rugged living coexists with strict spiritual traditions. As a highly agglutinative language with a rich vowel inventory (particularly the characteristic front vowel _ö_) and strict vowel harmony, Glömezher exhibits SOV (Subject-Object-Verb) word order and head-final features in its grammar, distinctly different from the southern Truskean. It possesses a complex noun system covering eight cases. This rigorous and precise internal linguistic logic stands in stark tension with the historically loose and free clan-based political structure of the Glömezher people. Yet, it has been preserved through a religious cultural tradition that venerates "Spirit-Speakers." Ultimately, with the establishment of the Moswarike dynasty, it evolved from a tribal dialect viewed as alien by settled peoples into an official language carrying the laws and spirituality of the steppe regime.

### [Laprio](拉普里奥语.md)

Laprio, as the traditional language of the Laprio people in the southern territories of the Trusk Empire, is a language imbued with a strong sense of classical ritual and theological dualism. Laprio, as the traditional language of the Laprio people in the southern territories of the Trusk Empire, is a language imbued with a strong sense of classical ritual and theological dualism. Its overall auditory impression is described as "crystal-clear" and distinctly demarcated, like the striking of crystal.

The core feature of this language lies in its rigorous "concordant morphological structure" (prefix for class + root + suffix for case). Its noun classification is not based on biological gender but on spatial perception and essential attributes: divided into the Li-class, representing the secular, artificial, and near, and the Na-class, representing the sacred, natural, and distant. This classification is directly fixed onto every noun via prefixes and also requires syntactic agreement in adjectives.

Syntactically, Laprio strictly follows SOV (Subject-Object-Verb) word order, in sharp contrast to Truskean's SVO. The verb must be placed at the end of the sentence as a "seal," giving the sentence a strong sense of closure and suspense. Although adjectives and subordinate clauses follow the noun, precise linkage between constituents is achieved through strict case suffixes (nominative/accusative/oblique).

> This is an ancient language that is gradually fading.

## 🌳 Ancestral Languages and Reconstruction

To clarify the genetic relationships among the three languages, linguists of the Royal Society have reconstructed their common ancestral nodes:
-   **[[Proto-Laaerad]]**: The common ancestor of all Laaerad languages, possessing highly complex morphological changes.
-   **[[Proto-Northeastern Laaerad]]**: The direct descendant of Proto-Laaerad in the northeastern region, serving as the missing link connecting the three aforementioned languages. Its reconstruction explains the reasons for the significant differences in cognates in later languages, such as "SVO vs. OV" and "agglutinative vs. analytic."
