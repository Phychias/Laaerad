# Andon

The concept of **Andon**, within the context of the [[../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index|Trusk Empire]], refers both to the mountainous region in the northwestern part of the empire (i.e., the [[俺东|Andon territory]]) and to the main ethnic group inhabiting this area. Andon and its people have played a significant role in the history of the [[../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index|Trusk Empire]], particularly in military conflicts and border relations. Their unique geographical environment and way of life have shaped their formidable national character.
