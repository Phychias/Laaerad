# Anioksait

**Anioksait** is a vast tropical rainforest region on the eastern continent of [Laaerad](docs/zh/地理与区域/拉埃拉德.md), forming the natural eastern boundary of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md).

## Introduction

**Anioksait** is renowned for its dense tropical rainforest ecosystem and serves as a natural barrier to the eastward territorial expansion of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md). Its unique geographical environment has played a significant role in the empire's history, limiting its further expansion.

## Geographical Features

**Anioksait** is located on the eastern border of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md) and is an extensive tropical rainforest. Its hot, humid climate and dense vegetation make the area difficult to traverse, forming a natural geographical obstacle.

## Historical Significance

In the history of the [Trusk Empire](../../国家与王朝/图斯克帝国/index.md), the **Anioksait Jungle** marked the limit of the empire's westward border expansion. During the [Heimet Dynasty](黑蔑特王朝.md) period, despite the empire's continuous efforts to expand its territory eastward, it was ultimately halted by the **Anioksait Jungle**, as the army could not advance further. This rainforest thus became a natural limit to the empire's territory, indirectly influencing its strategic layout and developmental direction.
